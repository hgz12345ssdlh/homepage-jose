个人简介 Introduction

Undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and Technology at ShanghaiTech University. My study is mainly focusing on Computer Systems Software. Specifically, my research interests span across parallel / distributed computing, modern operating systems (especially storage systems), high-performance computing, and cloud computing. I always enjoy building practical systems for large-scale real-world workloads that are not yet efficiently handled.

I have been working on several storage optimization projects with Prof. Shu Yin, in the Laboratory of Distributed I/O System and Data Science (L.I.O.N group), at ShanghaiTech University. Nowadays there exist many different data-analytic applications across various fields, interacting with large-scale & highly-structured data (e.g., ROS bag files, VMD molecule files, and cluster tracing logs). Such data are structured in very complicated ways. I/O with these data formats on traditional file systems is surprisingly inefficient. We build storage middle layers that are aware of these complicated application-specified data formats, trying to make use of their inner "content locality". Show More >>

I am also developing a scalable & affordable serverless graph convolutional networks computing system with Prof. Harry Xu, at University of California, Los Angeles. We are trying to integrate lambda computing with traditional graph computing frameworks, so that: 1. Split graph computation (e.g., aggregation) apart from tensor computation (e.g., matrix multiplication), use well-developed graph computing frameworks for the former part, and use parallelable computing resources for the latter part; 2. On the tensor part, enable modern serverless lambda computing as a cheaper and much more scalable alternative to traditional expensive GPUs. We hope to provide an affordable & efficient computing platform for such newly emerging graph neural networks, so that users can easily deploy graph ML applications and benefit from them.
